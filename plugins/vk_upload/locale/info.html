Plugin "to VK album"

The plugin uploads photos to the VK album. You can upload photos both to your albums and to albums of groups.
To be able to download, you need to log in. Click on the button and you will be taken to the program's website, where you will be asked to log in. Copy the received token to the program settings window.

Attention! To upload photos to the band's album, your account must have the rights to do so.
