Plugin "from Telegram group"

The plugin accepts all photos that are uploaded to a specific Telegram group.

In order to do this, get the key from the bot @photomachine_bot. Click on the button and you will go to the Telegram messenger. Using the bot is very easy:

  1. Click start or add. The bot will ask you to choose what you want to do - receive photos from the group or send photos to the group. Click "from group to photomachine"
  2. The bot will ask you to select a group to which it will be added.
  3. After selecting a group, the bot will give you a key that you need to enter into the plugin settings.
  4. Now, if someone adds a photo to the group, the plugin will receive it.

Attention! The plugin receives photos only when it is running! He will not receive photos that were added at a time when the program was not working.
