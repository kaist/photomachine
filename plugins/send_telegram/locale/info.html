Plug-in "to Telegram group"

The plugin uploads photos to the Telegram group.

In order to do this, get the key from the bot @photomachine_bot. Click on the button and you will go to the Telegram messenger. Using the bot is very easy:

  1. Click start or add. The bot will ask you to choose what you want to do - receive photos from the group or send photos to the group. Click "from photomachine to group"
  2. The bot will ask you to select a group to which it will be added.
  3. After selecting a group, the bot will give you a key that you need to enter into the plugin settings.
  4. Now the photos will be sent to this group.

Also, in the settings, you can specify a photo caption, and enable the ability to upload photos as a "document" (photos are not compressed)
